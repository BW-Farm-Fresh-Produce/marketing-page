About

Fresh Farm Produce is a state of the art technology that allows consumers and local farmers to connect in order to buy and sell fresh organic produce. This allows consumers to purchase local produce straight from the app and farmers to sell their produce to local consumers.

Fresh

Fresh, organic produce available to consumers.

Local

Connect with farmers in your area to purchase locally sourced organic produce.

Shop

Purchase or sell your fresh, local, organic produce without having to leave your house.
